The government of Mexico has implemented various measures to combat corruption, strengthen the rule of law, and promote transparency and efficiency in public administration. Legislative reforms have been enacted to support economic and social transformation. Efforts have been made to improve security, protect human rights, promote gender equality, and address drug addiction. The government has also focused on enhancing public security, defense capabilities, and disaster preparedness. Initiatives such as the establishment of the Guardia Nacional and the National Model of Police and Civic Justice have improved law enforcement coordination. The government is committed to creating safer communities and promoting international cooperation. Overall, Mexico is working towards inclusive development, justice, and the well-being of its population.'
